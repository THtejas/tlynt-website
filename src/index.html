TLYNT FAQs

  • How is TLYNT different from other casting platforms currently available?

    TLYNT is an app that connects advertisers directly with real-people talent. TLYNT will better serve both talent and advertisers through technology, artificial intelligence, and a modern, user-friendly interface that feels more like a dating app than a casting platform.

  • I’ve signed up for TLYNT! What’s next?

    After joining TLYNT, we will reach out to schedule a TLYNT photo shoot and capture the real YOU. We'll provide the professional studio, photographer, and lighting. You bring YOU!

  • Is there a TLYNT app available?

    We are busy developing the TYLNT app in preparation for a late-2022 launch. To receive the latest updates, please sign up at joinTLYNT.com.

  • I am an advertiser. How does TLYNT help me?

    When advertisers use TLYNT, they can select from a wide range of specifications based on their concept and immediately review matching profiles to make their casting selections.

  • Can people with managers/agents sign up for TLYNT?

    TLYNT is a platform that will work with people who currently have representation as well as those who do not.

  • Do you need any experience to sign up for TLYNT?

    No experience is necessary to join TLYNT.

  • Is TLYNT a paid service ?

    There is no fee to join TLYNT as an advertiser or talent at this time.

  • How exactly does talent get to know about upcoming opportunities?

    Talent will be notified of new opportunities through the TLYNT app.

  • Does it have castings for SAG/AFTRA actors?

    At this time, TLYNT will service non-union clients and productions. In the future, we plan to accommodate SAG/AFTRA actors.

  • Does TLYNT focus on all types of roles?

    TLYNT focuses on all types of roles, including lead and background for print and motion advertisements.
